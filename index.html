About Me

Hello! I’m Irwin Tay. I am passionate about Astrophysics and Data-driven Physics. As I pursue these passions, I ended up loving data analysis and machine learning work. As a result, I am constantly on a look out to improve my programming and coding skills in hopes of becoming a better data analyst where I don't lose out to others who have pursued a degree on that path.

I am a highly self-driven individual. Most of the coding skills I have learnt were all mainly outside my bachelor degree where I picked it up on my free time. With my research work experience, I am able to learn new things independently as well as quickly. This puts me advantageous in areas where things are highly-paced. Despite being able to work in highly-paced environments, I will still manage to make free time for myself.

Therefore, in my free time, I have a hobby for Photography - particularly Astrophotography. Not only that, I would like to go hiking as well as camping. A more sporty side of me is that I love to play basketball.

Currently I am enrolled into LMU MSc Astrophysics course and will be starting my semester in October 2023. In the meantime, I am open to finding part-time job on data analysis work.
